About LoopTailor

Our mission is to bring traditional tailoring businesses into the digital age, empowering them with efficient tools to grow.

Our Story

LoopTailor was born out of a simple observation: tailors and boutique owners, despite creating beautiful and intricate garments, often struggle with the back-office management of their businesses. Piles of paper measurement books, missed WhatsApp messages, and disorganized invoices were common pain points holding them back.

We developed a comprehensive management platform that acts as a digital ledger, an automated order tracker, and a customer relationship manager—all rolled into one intuitive application built for mobile and desktop.
