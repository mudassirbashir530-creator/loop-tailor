Refund Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

This Refund Policy outlines the terms and conditions for subscriptions on Loop Tailor.

1. Subscription Plans

We offer various subscription plans. Refunds are generally not provided for active subscriptions, except as required by law or under specific circumstances outlined below.

2. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation will prevent future billing, but no prorated refund will be issued for the current billing cycle.

3. Exceptions

If you experience significant technical issues that prevent you from using the core features of Loop Tailor, and our support team is unable to resolve them within a reasonable timeframe, you may be eligible for a refund at our discretion.
