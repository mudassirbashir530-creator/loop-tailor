Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2026

1. Subscription Cancellations

You may cancel your LoopTailor subscription at any time. Cancellations will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

2. Refunds

We do not offer pro-rated refunds for mid-billing cycle cancellations. However, if you experience significant technical issues that prevent you from using the service, please contact us for evaluation.

3. Evaluation Period

We recommend starting with our free or lowest-tier plan to evaluate the service before committing to a larger plan, ensuring it directly meets your tailoring business needs.
